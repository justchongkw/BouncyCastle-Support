Information We Collect

Bouncy Castle is designed to be simple and does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name or email to play. However, certain data may be collected automatically through third-party services we use:

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information, including your device's advertising identifier (such as the IDFA on iOS), device information, and general usage data, in order to serve and measure ads. This information may be used to show personalized or non-personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings.

Purchases (RevenueCat)

If you make an in-app purchase, such as removing ads, we use RevenueCat to process and manage that purchase. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related information and a non-personal app user identifier to validate and restore your purchases. Payment itself is handled securely by Apple; we do not receive or store your payment details. Learn more at RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to operate and improve the app, display advertising, and process purchases. We do not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Bouncy Castle is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention and Your Rights

We retain only the minimal data necessary to operate the app. Depending on your region, you may have rights to access or request deletion of your data. To make such a request, contact us at the email below.
